Description

This food is specifically made for Davas. Why? Because Davas are not like humans; they require a special type of nutrient which in the main element inside Dava Food.

Ingredients

For this dish we will need the following:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Dava Prism
  • Korean Ramen
  • Teriyaki Extract

Steps

  1. Find a small bowl
  2. Add everything inside the list unto the bowl
  3. Stir until mushy texture
  4. Microwave for ten hours*

*Use an industrial microwave since consumer level electronics have a strong limit regarding heating times.
